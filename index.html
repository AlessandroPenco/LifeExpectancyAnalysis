Life Expectancy Analysis

The life expectancy rate is a measure of the average number of years that a person is expected to live. It is an important indicator of a population's overall health and well-being.

Our plot shows the life expectancy rate for several countries around the world. As you can see, there is a wide range of rates, with some countries having much higher rates than others.

By analyzing this data, we can gain insight into factors that contribute to higher life expectancy rates, such as access to healthcare, education, and economic stability. We can also identify areas where improvement is needed in order to increase the life expectancy rate for all.
